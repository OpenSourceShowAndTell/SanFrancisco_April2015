What is Open Source Show & Tell!

We love Open Source software and so do you. Come celebrate hacker culture with Keen IO, Rainforest QA, and Segment (<< in their brand new shiny office!).

Open Source Show and Tell is an opportunity to share what you've been working on, learn about projects that your local community is supporting, and meet like minded people.

Keynote Speakers

  • Beth Massi // On Open Sourcing .NET
  • Beth is a Senior Program Manager on the Visual Studio team at Microsoft and a community champion for .NET developers, currently working on .NET Open Source and the .NET Foundation.
  • Russ Smith // On fourchette + developer workflow
  • Russell is the co-founder and CTO of Rainforest QA. He has tons of ops experience, mad coding skillz and 0 knowledge of geography. He loves hacking on open source, running meetups and arguing about startups.
  • Anthony Short // On the Deku project and composable UI.
  • Australian designer & front-end engineer living in San Francisco. Loves all things pirate and death metal. You can read his musings here.
  • Nahid Samsami // APIs and open source
  • Works on product at Keen IO with a focus on open source data tools. Cat advocate. Also known as Hidi.
