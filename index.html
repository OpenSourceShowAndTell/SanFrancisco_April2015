What is Open Source Show & Tell!

Open source show and tell is a half day event for developers to share, talk, and learn about cool open source projects. The event will be an opportunity for developers to share their own projects, discover new projects they can contribute to, and better understand the world of open source. Speakers and contributors will cover a variety of projects and content; from open sourcing internal tools, building a community and finding contributors, solving problems, and contribution etiquette.

Agenda

  • 1:30 pm - 2:00 pm: Show up, hang out, grab a seat!
  • 2:00 pm - 3:00 pm: Three 15 minute talks w/ 5 minutes of Q and A each.
  • 3:00 pm - 3:20 pm: Snack + drink break!
  • 3:20-3:40 pm: Talk w/ Q and A
  • 3:40 pm- 4:00 pm: Closing keynote: On open sourcing .NET
  • 4:00 pm - 5:00 pm: Open slots for anyone to demo/ talk about their open source project!
  • 5:00 pm - 6:00 pm: Happy Hour!!!
